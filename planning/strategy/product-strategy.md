# Product Strategy

## Purpose

Turn existing psychology blog traffic into a scalable monetization layer without changing the editorial mission of `click2pro.com`.

## Core Bet

Users who read high-intent psychology content often want deeper, more personalized interpretation than a blog post can provide. A focused assessment followed by a premium report is a cleaner monetization path than generic ads or low-context offers.

## Business Goals

- Monetize existing organic traffic with minimal manual blog maintenance
- Increase revenue per visitor through assessments, reports, bundles, and subscriptions
- Create reusable product infrastructure so new assessments can be launched quickly

## Product Principles

- Start with narrow, emotionally resonant assessment topics
- Match each assessment to clear blog intent
- Offer a meaningful free outcome before the paywall
- Keep report value concrete, personalized, and non-clinical

## Non-Goals For Initial Setup

- Full diagnosis workflows
- Therapist matching or treatment workflows
- Large custom CMS build before launch validation
- Manual editing of hundreds or thousands of blog pages

## Success Signals

- Popup click-through rate from blog traffic
- Assessment completion rate
- Free-to-paid report conversion rate
- Bundle take rate
- Subscription attach rate
- Top-performing blog topics by revenue per session
