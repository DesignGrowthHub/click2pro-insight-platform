# Pricing Model

## Pricing Objective

Create a clear ladder from free curiosity to premium report purchase and then to higher-value bundles or recurring access.

## Working Offer Ladder

- Free: assessment completion plus short result snapshot
- Single premium report: target range `$19-$29`
- 3-report bundle: target range `$39-$49`
- Monthly subscription: target range `$12-$19` per month

## Positioning Logic

- Single report is the easiest first purchase.
- Bundle serves users whose concerns overlap across topics.
- Subscription should feel useful only if it includes ongoing access, saved history, and additional assessments.

## Conversion Principles

- Show the free insight before presenting price.
- Anchor paid reports against the cost of staying confused, not against entertainment quizzes.
- Keep pricing simple at launch; avoid too many tiers.

## Questions To Validate Later

- Which assessment categories support the highest single-report conversion?
- Whether bundle pricing should be fixed or topic-based
- Whether subscription performs better as a post-purchase upsell than as a first offer
