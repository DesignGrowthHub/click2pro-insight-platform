# Homepage Copy

## Goal

Explain the platform in a premium, serious way and move users into the right assessment quickly.

## Hero

- Headline: Understand your patterns with more depth than a blog post can offer.
- Subheadline: Personalized insight reports for relationship dynamics, emotional patterns, burnout, identity conflict, and self-perception.
- Primary CTA: Start an Assessment
- Secondary CTA: Explore Report Topics

## Positioning Block

- The platform should feel like a guided insight experience, not a quiz gimmick.
- Language should promise clarity, structure, and emotional precision.

## How It Works

1. Choose a topic that matches what you are struggling with.
2. Complete a focused assessment in a few minutes.
3. Unlock a structured insight report tailored to your response pattern.

## Featured Categories

- Relationships and attachment
- Self-worth and imposter patterns
- Burnout, motivation, and emotional detachment
- Identity, conflict, and recovery

## Trust Notes

- Not a diagnosis tool
- Built for reflection and pattern awareness
- Designed for people who want more than generic advice

## Conversion Notes

- Homepage should support both cold traffic and blog-referred traffic.
- Blog-referred traffic should ideally land deeper in a topic-specific flow when possible.
