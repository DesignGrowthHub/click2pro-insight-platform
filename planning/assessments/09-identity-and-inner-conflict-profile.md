# Identity & Inner Conflict Profile

## User Problem

The user feels internally split, uncertain, or inconsistent and wants clearer structure around the tensions shaping self-concept and decision-making.

## Entry Topics

- Identity conflict
- Inner contradiction
- Sense of self confusion
- Life-direction uncertainty

## Free Result Angle

Reveal whether the conflict centers on role pressure, values mismatch, self-suppression, or competing internal identities.

## Paid Report Angle

Unpack the user's internal tension map, recurring self-division patterns, and points of coherence.

## Key Dimensions

- Role strain
- Values conflict
- Authenticity suppression
- Decision paralysis
- Self-definition clarity
