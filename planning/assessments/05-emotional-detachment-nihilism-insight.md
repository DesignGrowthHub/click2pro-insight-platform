# Emotional Detachment / Nihilism Insight

## User Problem

The user feels emotionally flat, disconnected, or existentially indifferent and wants to understand whether the pattern reflects protection, burnout, hopelessness, or loss of meaning.

## Entry Topics

- Emotional detachment
- Nihilism
- Feeling numb
- Meaninglessness

## Free Result Angle

Identify whether the current state looks more defensive, exhausted, disillusioned, or chronically disengaged.

## Paid Report Angle

Clarify the user's emotional shutdown pattern, meaning loss signals, and re-engagement entry points.

## Key Dimensions

- Emotional access
- Future orientation
- Sense of meaning
- Energy availability
- Social withdrawal
