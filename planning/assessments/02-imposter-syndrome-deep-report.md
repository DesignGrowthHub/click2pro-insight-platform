# Imposter Syndrome Deep Report

## User Problem

The user feels fraudulent, undeserving, or chronically unconvinced by their own competence despite evidence of ability.

## Entry Topics

- Imposter syndrome
- High-achiever self-doubt
- Fear of being exposed

## Free Result Angle

Identify whether the pattern looks like perfectionism, comparison distortion, praise rejection, or fear-based overcompensation.

## Paid Report Angle

Explain confidence distortions, trigger contexts, and the user's likely internal validation pattern.

## Key Dimensions

- Achievement discounting
- Fear of exposure
- Praise discomfort
- Comparison intensity
- Performance pressure
