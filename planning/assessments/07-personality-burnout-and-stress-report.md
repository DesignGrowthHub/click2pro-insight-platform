# Personality Burnout & Stress Report

## User Problem

The user is functioning under pressure but feels overextended, brittle, or chronically depleted and wants to understand how personality style may be amplifying burnout.

## Entry Topics

- Burnout
- Chronic stress
- High-functioning exhaustion
- Personality-driven overload

## Free Result Angle

Indicate whether the stress pattern is driven more by perfectionism, hyper-responsibility, emotional labor, or identity tied to productivity.

## Paid Report Angle

Explain the user's burnout style, hidden load sources, and why rest alone may not be resolving the problem.

## Key Dimensions

- Pressure source
- Recovery difficulty
- Over-functioning pattern
- Emotional labor load
- Identity-performance overlap
