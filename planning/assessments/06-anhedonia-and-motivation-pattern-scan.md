# Anhedonia & Motivation Pattern Scan

## User Problem

The user notices that pleasure, interest, and drive have thinned out and wants a more nuanced read on what that pattern may reflect.

## Entry Topics

- Anhedonia
- No motivation
- Nothing feels enjoyable
- Loss of interest

## Free Result Angle

Show whether the pattern is closer to depletion, overstress, emotional flattening, or chronic disengagement.

## Paid Report Angle

Map the user's motivation blockers, reward disconnection, and the environments where energy drops fastest.

## Key Dimensions

- Interest loss
- Reward responsiveness
- Effort aversion
- Routine collapse
- Recovery capacity
